Welcome to Coffee Party

Your premium coffee delivery and event specialist

Who Are We?

Coffee Party is a young and innovative company dedicated to bringing exceptional coffee experiences directly to businesses and individuals. Founded in 2020, we've grown from a small startup to become one of Sweden's leading coffee delivery and event services.

Our team consists of certified baristas, coffee enthusiasts, and logistics experts who share a passion for quality coffee. We believe that every cup tells a story, and we're here to help you discover yours.

What Do We Do?

We specialize in three core services: delivering premium coffee machines to offices, providing fresh coffee bean subscriptions, and hosting unforgettable coffee tasting events. Whether you need a reliable espresso machine for your workplace or want to organize a unique coffee party, we've got you covered.

Our coffee selection features beans from sustainable farms across the world, including Ethiopia, Colombia, Brazil, and Guatemala. Each variety is carefully selected and roasted to perfection, ensuring an exceptional taste experience.

Why Us?

Premium Quality

We source only the finest coffee beans from certified sustainable farms. Each batch is quality-tested and freshly roasted to ensure maximum flavor and aroma in every cup.

Fast Delivery

Same-day delivery available in Stockholm and next-day delivery across Sweden. Our efficient logistics ensure your coffee machines and beans arrive fresh and on time, every time.

Expert Knowledge

Our certified baristas bring years of experience and passion. We provide comprehensive training, maintenance support, and expert advice to enhance your coffee experience.
